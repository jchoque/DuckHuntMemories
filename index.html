Duck Hunt Memories

  • High Concept: A game to remember the very popular duck hunt NES.
  • Player Experience Goals: Experience good memories about this game.
  • Genre: Kids
  • Game play: Simply aim with the pointer and shoot by clicking. Try to get the ducks!
  • Prototype goal: Kill as many ducks as you can before you run out of cannon balls.
  • Features: Same sounds and effects as in the original game.
  • Setting/Premise: When I was 4, NES became a very important part of my childhood. Games like Duck Hunt will never be forgotten.

  • Target Audience: Kids around the age of 4
  • Play time: Around 2 minutes
